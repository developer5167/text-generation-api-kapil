1.How to make payments?
A)Customer can make payments through mobile apps, the web portal, and web applications.

  Subscriber App:
  ---------------
   Joined customers can make payments to My Chits through the Subscriber App.
   
   Subscribers can join another chit and make payments using the ‘Join Chit’ option.
 
  Collection App:
  ---------------
   Without joining, customers can make payments for new enrollment, and after enrollment, the payments are transferred to the respective Chit Group and AR Group.

   Joined customers can make payments through agents listed in the chit. Agents can make payments on behalf of the selected customer and their related customers.

  Subscriber Portal:
  ------------------
   Each subscriber logs in with their mobile number and can make payments through the ‘My Chits’ option.
   
   Subscribers can join another chit and make payments using the ‘Join Chit’ option.


  Note: Subscriber can make the payments single chit or multiple chit's.




2.What payment methods are accepted?
A)Cash, online, and QR code payment methods are allowed.

  App's:
  ------
   Cash, Online Payment are allowed.

  Subscriber Portal:
  ------------------
    Cash Free & world line payment are allowed.
  
  Web Application:
  ----------------
    Cash, Bank(Cheque, Online, Debit & Credit) type of methods are allowed.
