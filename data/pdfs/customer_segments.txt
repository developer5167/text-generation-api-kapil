YOUNG & FIRST-TIME INVESTORS:
Target: Students, young professionals starting their career
Investment: Small monthly amounts
Purpose: Build long-term savings foundation
Benefits: 
  - Fund higher education
  - Meet emergency expenses  
  - Fulfill life goals like buying car, home, or funding marriage
Recommended Plans: Mini Chit Plan, Family Secure Plan

WORKING PROFESSIONALS:
Target: Employed individuals with stable income
Investment: Medium to large monthly amounts  
Purpose: Financial planning for future expenses
Benefits:
  - Avoid high-interest bank loans
  - Use savings to pre-close other loans
  - Secure retirement corpus
  - Children's education funding
Recommended Plans: Gold Future Plan, Business Growth Plan
