- No Collateral: Borrow without providing security or guarantors
- Easy Access: Simple documentation and quick disbursal

- Financial Discipline: Encourages regular monthly savings habit
- Habit Building: Develops long-term financial planning skills
- Goal Orientation: Helps save for specific life goals

- Quick Liquidity: Faster access to funds compared to bank loans

- Risk Management: Lower risk compared to stock market investments
- Stable Returns: Predictable returns without market volatility

- Safety: Registered companies provide legal protection

- Interest Rates: Lower borrowing costs than personal loans
- Processing: Faster than bank loan approvals
- Documentation: Simpler than bank loan applications

- Students: Education funding with family support
- Homemakers: Safe savings with emergency access
